Hogwarts Acceptance Form

A project created for FreeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design - building a survey form.

Responsive Web Design

Front-End

As a Harry Potter fan, I reimagined the project as "What if Hogwarts embraced technology and send their potential student a QR code that links to a web form along with their acceptance letter? What would that form look like?" Instead of students sending in owls with their notice of acceptance, this form will act as their virtual owl.

Tools used

  • Responsive Design
  • HTML5/CSS3
