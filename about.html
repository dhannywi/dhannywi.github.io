I am diligently working towards attaining a Master of Science in Information Science, anticipated to culminate in December 2023, with a keen interest in machine learning and web development.

Transitioning from a background in design to the realm of software engineering was a challenging, yet rewarding journey. With an inherent passion for perpetual learning, I derive immense satisfaction from unraveling complex problems. I seek opportunities to advance my skills and make meaningful contributions.

Currently, I serve as a Research Software Engineering/Scientist at Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC). My primary focus revolves around developing functionalities for the Machine Learning Hub web application, which seamlessly integrates into the Tapis framework. This dynamic platform is designed to streamline the machine learning workflow for researchers utilizing TACC's high-performance computing systems, exemplifying our commitment to enhancing their experience. This endeavor extends from the project I undertook as a Software Engineering Intern at SGX3.

Beyond this, I have also had the privilege of serving as a Teaching Assistant for the "Programming for Informatics" course, leveraging my expertise to mentor and guide a diverse group of more than 50 students through their Python programming assignments and projects.

In my downtime, I enjoy hiking, kayaking, practicing yoga, and rock climbing.

My coursework includes: Software Engineering and Design, Computer Vision, Human-AI Interaction, Predictive Analytics and Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence in Health, Data Wrangling, Introduction to Machine Learning, Introduction to Scientific Programming, and Data Storytelling.

Fun fact: I worked as a Fashion Designer and Product Developer before I fell in love with coding 😊
